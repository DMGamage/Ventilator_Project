Please note:
The use of a small minimum radius ( 3 < rmin < 6 ) is suitable for 1-cycle camshaft, but it may result in sharp shapes for 2 or 3-cycle cams.
For 2 and 3 cycles camshafts, make sure the minimum radius defined by h2 and hm is big enough to ensure a proper soft shape.
